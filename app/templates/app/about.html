About EME Ready Mix

Company History

EME Ready Mix was founded in 2019 by Eduardo Luna and Manuel Duran, with the vision to become a premier ready mix concrete provider to the construction boom in the Austin Metropolitan Area. Eduardo and Manuel have a combined 50 years of experience in the construction industry providing logistics and contracting services to a myriad of construction projects. Additionally, they have built a network of construction professionals who support EME Ready Mix and supply the company with material, trucks, and equipment. The founders of EME Ready Mix have been working hard to establish a recognized company that provides concrete services to construction projects.

What sets EME Ready Mix apart from the competition is the timely, accurate, and friendly service that it has been providing clients since the beginning.

Industries We Serve

Our concrete experience includes residential projects such as home foundations, patios, pools, sidewalks, driveways, multi-family dwellings, and concrete slabs. We also have extensive experience providing ready mix concrete services to commercial projects such as retail, manufacturing facilities, automotive shops, higher education, restaurants, and sporting complexes. EME Ready Mix is an experienced company that has the infrastructure to take on a concrete project of any size.
