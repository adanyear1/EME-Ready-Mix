Concrete Slabs & Foundations

A strong foundation is necessary for a building to stay in position, which is why concrete is the ideal material. Concrete foundations are ideal because they can resist pressure from the water and the soil from the outside. A benefit of having a concrete foundation is that concrete pours can be adapted to all foundation designs. We have over 15 years of experience in providing concrete foundations for both residential and commercial buildings.

Driveways & Walkways

The first thing you set foot on when you arrive to your home is the driveway. Have you ever thought about the stress your driveway is exposed to? A driveway is expected to support two to four vehicles, withstand intense weather conditions such as freezes and extreme summers, and provide an aesthetic look to a home.

A Walkway is a path used by individuals, scooter riders, and cyclists. Due to the increased use of electric bikes and scooters, sidewalks are taking on more stress due to the combined traffic. More than ever, you need an experience concrete contractor to help you build your walkway to your standards, and that will be able to adapt to the future use.

At EME Ready Mix we understand the importance of using high quality concrete for driveways and walkways, which is why our ready-mix product is made using strict guidelines. Our team has experience working with driveways for ranches, homes in neighborhoods, and commercial driveway projects. We have also worked on walkway projects for neighborhoods, urban centers, retail centers, and parks.

Patios & Porches

After a long day at work, you want to relax in your front yard and enjoy your evening, or on a hot summer day you want to have a bar-b-q with your friends and family. Concrete is an excellent material for your patio or porch because of its toughness and ability to withstand the elements. Our poured concrete patios will transform your backyard to a beautiful area that you will enjoy for years to come. The benefits of using concrete for your patio or porch is that it is low maintenance, reduces gaps that lead to infestations and is adaptable to your design. We have experience pouring for stamped concrete, stone, brick patios, and tile patios and porches.
